Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the PolySignal Telegram bot (@PolySignalAlertsBot, the “Bot”) collects, why, and how it is handled. PolySignal (“we”, “us”) is the operator of the Bot. By starting and using the Bot you agree to this Policy.

1. What we collect

When you use the Bot, we collect and store:

  • Telegram identifiers — your Telegram user ID and, if set, your Telegram username. We do not collect your phone number, contacts, or real name.
  • Account & subscription data — your subscription tier and its expiry date.
  • Usage & preferences — the Polymarket wallet addresses you choose to follow, your category filters, daily-digest settings, custom alert rules, and referral relationships.
  • Activity log — a record of in-Bot events (for example: starting the Bot, following a wallet, reaching a plan limit, starting a checkout) used for analytics, product improvement and abuse prevention.
  • Payment records — for purchases made with Telegram Stars: the transaction identifier, amount, currency, date and the payment confirmation provided by Telegram.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • We never ask for or store cryptocurrency private keys or seed phrases. The Bot is read-only and cannot move funds.
  • We do not receive or store your card or bank details. Telegram Stars payments are processed entirely by Telegram.
  • We do not read your other Telegram chats or messages — only the commands and taps you send to the Bot.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate the Bot and deliver the alerts and information you request.
  • To manage subscriptions, plan limits and referral rewards.
  • To prevent abuse and apply rate limits.
  • To analyse usage in aggregate and improve the product.
  • To meet legal, accounting and tax obligations.

4. Third parties

We rely on the following service providers to run the Bot:

  • Telegram — the messaging platform that delivers the Bot and processes Telegram Stars payments, under Telegram’s own privacy terms.
  • Railway — cloud infrastructure that hosts the application and its database.
  • Sentry — error monitoring, which may incidentally process technical data when an error occurs.

The Bot also reads public market and on-chain data from Polymarket’s API and the Polygon blockchain. This is public information about wallets and markets — we do not send your personal data to those sources. We do not sell your data, and we do not share it for advertising.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and usage data for as long as you use the Bot. Payment records are retained as required for accounting and legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see “Your rights” below); some records may be retained where the law requires it.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. In practice you can:

  • remove followed wallets and change your settings at any time inside the Bot;
  • stop using the Bot at any time;
  • request access to, or full deletion of, your data by contacting us (see below).

7. Data security

We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The Bot is not directed to anyone under the age of 18, or under the age of majority in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of the Bot after a change means you accept the updated Policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy question or request, contact us through the bot — open @PolySignalAlertsBot in Telegram and send a message.

PolySignal is an information service. It does not trade, hold keys or provide financial advice. This Privacy Policy covers data handling only — see the Terms of Service for the terms of use.
